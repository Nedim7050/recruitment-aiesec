About AIESEC Carthage

AIESEC Carthage is the local chapter of the world's largest youth-run organization, founded in 1962 and based in Tunisia. We are a dynamic community of young leaders committed to developing leadership potential through international exchanges, cultural understanding, and global impact initiatives. Our members come from diverse backgrounds and universities across Tunisia, united by a shared vision of creating positive change in our communities and beyond.

Through our programs, we connect Tunisian youth with global opportunities, foster cross-cultural understanding, and develop the next generation of responsible leaders who will shape a better future for Tunisia and the world.
